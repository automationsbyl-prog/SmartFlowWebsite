Common questions

FAQ

How long does a typical project take?

Duration depends on project scope. Smaller optimizations can be completed in 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive digitalization projects typically take 6-12 weeks. We'll give you a realistic estimate in our first conversation.

What company sizes is SmartFlow suitable for?

We specialize in small and medium-sized businesses - from tradespeople with 3 employees to regional companies. Our solutions are specifically designed for this target group and affordable.

What does consulting cost?

The initial consultation is free. After that, we create an individual offer based on your needs. Our packages start at €500 for smaller optimizations.

Do we need to switch existing software?

Not necessarily. We first analyze whether your existing tools can be used optimally. Often, better configuration or interfaces can achieve significant improvements without introducing completely new software.

How does an initial consultation work?

In the free initial consultation (approx. 30 minutes, by phone or video) we get to know your situation: Which processes are currently problematic? What are your goals? Then we assess if and how we can help.
